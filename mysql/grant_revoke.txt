查看所有用户
SELECT DISTINCT CONCAT('User: ''',user,'''@''',host,''';') AS query FROM mysql.user;
查看用户的权限
show grants for 'user'@'localhost';

revoke 跟 grant 的语法差不多，只需要把关键字 “to” 换成 “from” 即可：
grant all on *.* to dba@localhost;
revoke all on *.* from dba@localhost;
